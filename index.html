A quick introduction.

I'm a data professional pursuing an MS in Operations Research at Columbia University, with an honours BSc in Mathematics & Statistics from the University of Toronto. My work sits at the intersection of analytics, experimentation, and decision support — translating messy, large-scale data into clear answers business teams can act on.

I've built KPI dashboards and automated reporting pipelines that shipped to operations, research, and product teams — most recently at Alibaba Cloud, where I worked with 300K+ daily order records to surface exceptions and tighten SLA tracking. I'm comfortable in SQL, Python, R, and Tableau/Power BI, and I care about the last mile — making insights legible to non-technical stakeholders.

Outside of work I'm drawn to problems where the answer isn't obvious from the raw numbers — funnel diagnostics, A/B test design, and the kind of analysis where framing matters as much as math.
